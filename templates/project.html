Smart Irrigation

It is an IOT based smart irrigation system. It is a Django application (deployed on digital ocean droplet) which assists the farmer with mindful usage of water for irrigation by analyzing the data through sensors placed in the field through the app and sms features. Aim to add prediction of soil moisture powered by machine learning for effective irrigation management system.
